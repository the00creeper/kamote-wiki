# ⏮️ Our Origins

Kamote Server was founded by **Shinjiru** and **.RyNymph** on March 31, 2025. It was originally a private Aternos server for the two founders, until **DesiAndDevo** joined the fun via their Facebook livestream. She suggested creating server content on [TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server), where the server's name reached heights.

{% hint style="info" %}
The passage below is from **Shinjiru’s** [Discord message](https://discordapp.com/channels/1362052421261590732/1362052421907644509/1393829885293887561) narrating the server's origins, dated July 13, 2025.
{% endhint %}

> Noong **March 31**, inaya ko si **Lou** (.RyNymph) sa bahay namin kasi may keyboard ako (piano) and interested daw siyang matuto. So pumunta siya dito at nananghalian muna kami. After non naglaro muna kami ng Genshin, farm farm, flex flex ganiyan. Napakat na kami sa paglalaro and since naglalaro na rin naman na kami, biglang sumagi sa isip ko yung Minecraft kasi matagal na rin kaming hindi nakakalaro ulit. Pabiro kong sinabi, “Gusto mo Minecraft na lang tayo eh,” eto namang si **Lou** na uto, “Sige oh!?”
>
>
>
> Gumawa kami ngayon ng simpleng Aternos server para sa aming dalawa at kinailangan namin ng Bedrock crossplay plugin na GeyserMC para makapaglaro kami kasi Bedrock siya sa phone at Java ako sa luma kong laptop. Natapos guys yung buong araw, hindi na kami nakatugtog ng piano, and in the following days nag-grind na lang kami nang nag-grind.
>
>
>
> April 12 noong nakabili ako ng una kong microphone and naisip namin ni Lou na mag-live sa FB. Edi nag-live kami for the first time noong April 15, saktong-sakto din sa Holy Week kaya maraming nanonood na FB friends ko, pero mukhang iilan lang sa kanila ang interested sa Minecraft at isa na don si **eri** (DesiAndDevo). Nag-join siya kinabukasan, nag-enjoy, at plinanong mas ipakilala yung server sa mundo.
>
>
>
> In-encourage ako ni eri na gamitin na yung platform na TikTok, kasi maniniwala ba kayo guys na buong buhay ko ni-minsan hindi ko in-install yang app na yan? HAHAHA. Ang tingin ko kasi sa TikTok ay parang brainrot app na nilalason utak ng kabataan with some useless shit videos. Pero kinonvince ako ni eri otherwise so I gave it a try, wala namang masama.
>
>
>
> In-upload namin yung unang video about sa constuction ng Ericka Avenue and hindi siya bumenta, pero nung prinomote namin umabot naman ng 1K views. Naging workflow ko yung magre-record ng video, mawawala ng ilang araw para mag-edit, magpo-post, tas magre-record ulit. Na-realize kong nauubos yung oras ko sa edit and hindi na ako nakakalaro; I spend more time editing videos than actually playing the game, but I think it was worth kasi may ilang positive comments na akong natatanggap sa mga viewers, so I kept going.
>
>
>
> Overtime, dumami yung mga comments na, “Pwede po mag join,” “Kuya how to join po,” so we made a big move to open the server to the public. I spent the first two weeks of May figuring out how to create a Discord server (I had zero prior knowledge) and finding the perfect plugins that would still make the server feel vanilla. Then on May 17, the server was finally open, with **Minji** being the first joiner (may video tungkol lang sa kaniya [dito](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server/video/7509551244696997127)).
>
>
>
> On the first week of June, I discovered the economy and rank plugins we know today. Inaral ko siya i-setup and gamitin for a week. I built my first budget PC on June 15 and saktong sakto siya dahil kailangan ko ng more computing power kung seryoso ako sa pagma-manage ng server. I can finally use shaders without lag, a dream I've been carrying from childhood. The three tutorial videos na nasa TikTok ngayon, I spent two weeks shooting them with Lou and actually editing them.
>
>
>
> On the night of June 27, I posted the three videos [_Claiming_](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server/video/7521377680189607176), [_Economy_](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server/video/7521385347972926738), and [_Ranking_](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server/video/7521392157417098514) simultaneously and ang pinakasumabog sa kanila ay yung [_Economy_](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server/video/7521385347972926738). Kinabukasan nagulat ako bakit biglang dumami yung sumasali sa server, sunod-sunod, then tiningnan ko yung views nung [_Economy_](https://www.tiktok.com/@kamote.server/video/7521385347972926738) 13K views!? Partida wala pa yung promote. I guess TikTok has its mysterious ways in manipulating the algorithm.
>
>
>
> And the rest is history 😁
