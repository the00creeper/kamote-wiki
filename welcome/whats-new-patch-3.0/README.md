# ✨ What's New (Patch 3.0)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/kamote5.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Season 3 Update Overview 🌱

Welcome to **Season 3** of  **Kamote Server**! This season focuses on **long-term balance, stronger infrastructure, a healthier economy, and fair play**, while preserving the creativity and community-driven gameplay that defines our server.

Below is a complete list of changes, **ordered by importance**, with explanations to help you understand _why_ these updates exist and how they benefit everyone.

***

## 🚨 Major Gameplay & Economy Changes &#x20;

### ✈️ Elytras Disabled (Season 3 World) &#x20;

Elytras are now **disabled in the Season 3 world**. This change is meant to:

* Encourage meaningful travel choices
* Promote infrastructure, rail networks, and transport hubs
* Make distance and geography matter again

**Balancing change:**

* Minecart speed has been **tripled**, from **8 blocks/sec → 24 blocks/sec**

We expect railways, stations, and community transport projects to flourish as a result.

### 🛠️ Mending Disabled&#x20;

The **Mending enchantment** has been fully disabled to keep the economy active and resource-driven. Tools and armor are now meant to be _used_, repaired, and replaced—keeping mining, trading, and crafting relevant.

**Balancing changes:**

* **Anvil enchanting now always costs only 1 level, regardless of the number of anvil uses**
* Vanilla repair mechanics remain unchanged

This ensures repairs are still accessible without allowing infinite item lifespans.

### 💎 Diamonds Now Auto-Convert to Money

Diamonds are now **automatically converted into money when mined**. This prevents tax evasion through diamond hoarding and keeps the economy transparent.

**New command:**

* `/dcraft` — craft diamond tools, armor, and duplicate netherite upgrade templates using money

Diamonds remain valuable—but now function as a true currency rather than a stockpile.

### 🎰 Gambling System Adjustments

Due to diamond auto-conversion, gambling mechanics have been updated.

**New system:**

* `/bet <amount>` withdraws **temporary gambling diamonds**
* Gambling diamonds:
  * Cannot be stored in containers
  * Must be dropped directly into the machine hopper

**Important notes:**

* Rewards must now be handled via **command blocks**
* Existing machines may require redesigns

📩 **Contact an admin** before building a gambling machine—we’re happy to help ensure it works properly.

## 💰 Economy and Community

### 📉 Reduced Taxes (Across the Board)

Taxes have been **cut in half** to ease economic pressure:

* **Balance tax:** reduced from **2% → 1%** of player balance
* **Claim tax:** now **$1 per 2,048 active claim blocks** (previously $1 per 1,024)

This makes expansion and savings more sustainable for everyone.

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Familias Weekly Allowance

Active **familias** may now receive a **weekly allowance** sourced from **50% of the weekly tax pool**.

* Only familias that meet activity and eligibility requirements qualify
* Encourages cooperation, shared goals, and long-term communities

📖 See the **Familia Weekly Allowance** page for full requirements and details.

## 🏷️ Ranks & Perks Updates

### 🍍 Rank Rebranding (Filipino Fruit Theme)

Ranks have been renamed to better match the Kamote Server identity:

| Old Rank  | New Rank      |
| --------- | ------------- |
| \[NEW]    | **\[KAMOTE]** |
| \[PLAYER] | **\[SAGING]** |
| \[PRO]    | **\[MANGGA]** |
| \[VIP]    | **\[PINYA]**  |

Is it _giving_ or not _giving_? 😄

### 🌟 New Supporter Ranks

Two optional supporter ranks are now available:

* **\[BOOSTER]** — Discord Server Boosters
* **\[DONATOR]** — Monthly Server Donators

**Important:**

* Requires at least **\[MANGGA]** rank
* Does **not** affect rank progression
* All perks are **quality-of-life or cosmetic only**
* No pay-to-win advantages

### ⏱️ Command Cooldown Adjustments

* `/home` cooldown for **\[MANGGA]** increased: **20 → 40 minutes**
* `/home` and `/tpa` now have **separate cooldown timers**

### 🧢 Perk Changes

* `/hat` has been moved from **\[PRO]** to **\[BOOSTER]**
* `/warp` command removed since all players can access all warps
* `[PINYA]` and higher ranks can now **set multiple homes** to replace the function of `/warp`

### 🎖️ Rank Promotion Rewards Removed

The **$50 reward** for role rank promotions (e.g., Ranger V → Ranger IV) has been removed.

* Promotions are now **purely cosmetic**
* Progression focuses on prestige rather than farming money

📖 See the **Ranks and Commands** page for full details.

## 🌍 World & Progression Updates

### 🌋 Nether & End (Temporarily Closed)

The **Nether and End remain closed** in the Season 3 world for now.

This is intentional to:

* Prevent early overgrinding
* Allow the overworld economy to stabilize

These dimensions will open soon—stay tuned for announcements.

### 🕰️ Season 1 ↔ Season 3 Portal

A permanent portal now connects **Season 1** and **Season 3** worlds.

**Season 1 World Rules:**

* Read-only: no building, breaking, or modifying
* Intended for **touring, history, and showcases**
* Elytras enabled
* `/kit elytra` available for easy exploration
* New players may use `/home` and `/warp` **within Season 1 only**

**Restrictions:**

* No cross-world `/home` usage
* You cannot teleport _from_ Season 3 _to_ Season 1 homes (or vice versa)

{% hint style="info" %}
Elytras remain **enabled in the Season 1 world** for touring and nostalgia.
{% endhint %}

### 🔥 PvP & Environmental Changes

To increase challenge and realism:

* **PvP is now enabled inside claims (but are still illegal!)**
* Creepers and endermen can grief
* Fire now spreads

This keeps the fun of creepers exploding! 💥

### 🧱 Claim Block Cap

Players may now accumulate a **maximum of 4,096 claim blocks**, regardless of playtime.

This ensures:

* Fair land distribution
* Reduced land hoarding
* More room for new players to settle

***

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Fair Play & Account Security

### 🔒 One Account Per Player

Players are now limited to **one Minecraft account per person**.

* Prevents AFK farming and alt abuse
* Ensures a fair economy

All new players must **link their Minecraft account to Discord** before joining for the first time.
