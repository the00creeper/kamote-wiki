The current economy is not functioning well, so the team discussed ways to make it relevant again.

## Season 5 Proposals

1. No More Central Currency ✅
   1. Shops will no longer exist. A mail system will be introduced for easier trading.
2. No More Land Claims ✅
   1. Kamorte Suprema will still exist to maintain order.
   2. No more commands; the server will go fully vanilla.
3. No More Ranks ✅
   1. Ranks create issues with monetization.
4. 24-Hour Cooldown Upon Death ❌
5. No More Coordinates ❌
   1. To encourage players to develop a sense of direction.
   2. Map mods or any mods that assist navigation will not be allowed.

### Suggestions and Reactions to the Proposals

Proposal 1: No More Central Currency ✅

* No objections or suggestions were raised.

Proposal 2: No More Land Claims / Full Vanilla ✅

* Setsu: Suggested manual investigation for stealing incidents. Introduce a manual justice system with no teleportation; offenders must be caught by police in-game like in real life. _(Already scrapped.)_ ❌
* Setsu and Dopa: Suggested removing CoreProtect inspect functions. ❌

Proposal 3: No More Ranks ✅

* Donators may receive limited access to `/home` and `/tpa`. _(No concrete decision yet.)_ ⏳
* Lei: Suggested one centralized legitimate villager trading area, with villagers outside spawn being illegal. _(Already scrapped.)_ ❌

Proposal 4: 24-Hour Cooldown Upon Death ❌

* Majority strongly disagreed.&#x20;
* Reason: Not all players are skilled enough for this rule, especially many casual players joining the server.&#x20;

Proposal 5: No More Coordinates ❌

* Loweee, Setsu, and Dopamine disagreed.
* Reason: Not all players are skilled enough to navigate without tools.
* Coordinates are essential and have many important uses for players, making them difficult to remove.
