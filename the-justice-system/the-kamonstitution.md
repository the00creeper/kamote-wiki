# 📜 The Kamonstitution

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (3).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**The 2025 Kamonstitution** embodies the foundational laws and ideals of Kamote Server. Established within are our commitment to the **vanilla Minecraft experience** with light, player-driven economics, and a strong emphasis on community, fairness, and due process. Defined are the structure of **justice** through courts and trials, protects **player rights** and property, and outlines **responsible use** of land and technology. The **Kamonstitution**, through its articles, ensures order, sustainability, and meaningful player interaction **over status or power**.

<h2 align="center">PREAMBLE</h2>

Panatang maka-ML, iniibig ko ang jungle monsters: ang crab sa top at bottom lane, gusto kong&#x20;mapasaakin upang maging malakas at mabilis na lumevel-up. Bilang ganti, papatayin ko lahat&#x20;ng kalaban, at wawasakin ko ang tore nilang madaanan. Pupuntahan ko ang kasamang&#x20;humihingi ng tulong, at tutunawin ko ang kalabang makakasalubong. Babantayan ko ang tore&#x20;namin, at buong-tapang na makikipaglaban nang walang pag-aalinlangan. Sisikapin kong&#x20;maging isang tunay na ML Player—maka-triple kill, maniac, at savage.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE I: DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES</h2>

_Section 1._ Kamote Server is a semi-vanilla, semi-economy Minecraft survival multiplayer server, accessible to both the Java and Bedrock versions of the game.

_Section 2._ Kamote Server shall remain primarily a vanilla Minecraft experience, utilizing only select plugins that enhance quality of life and administrative oversight. More powerful quality of life commands are reserved for higher ranks to preserve the vanilla Minecraft experience for new players.

_Section 3._ The economy shall be light and player-driven, with diamonds serving as currency. No command-based marketplace shall exist to preserve organic trade between players.

_Section 4._ Kamote Server values the relationships and interactions players establish with each other above any material or rank-based achievements. The community shall be built on cooperation, fairness, and mutual respect.

_Section 5._ Civilian authority is, at all times, supreme over the military. The Kamote National Police is the protector of the people and the State. Its goal is to secure the sovereignty of the State and the integrity of the national territory.

_Section 6._ The prime duty of the Administrative Council is to serve and protect the people. &#x20;

_Section 7._ The maintenance of peace and order, the protection of life, liberty, and property, and the promotion of the general welfare are essential for the enjoyment by all players.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE II: PLAYER RIGHTS</h2>

_Section 1._ No player shall be deprived of life, liberty, or property without due process of law, nor shall any person be denied the equal protection of the laws.

_Section 2._ The abrupt killing of a player by another player in any dimension shall be prohibited, with exceptions granted when the killed player has given explicit permission to be killed or when an agreement between the players to engage in killing for sport has been made.

_Section 3_. (1) No person shall be held to answer for a criminal offense without due process of law.

(2)               In all criminal prosecutions, the accused shall be presumed innocent until the contrary is proved, and shall enjoy the right to be heard by himself and counsel, to be informed of the nature and cause of the accusation against him, to have a speedy, impartial, and public trial, to meet the witnesses face to face, and to have compulsory process to secure the attendance of witnesses and the production of evidence in his behalf. &#x20;

_Section 4._ (1) Players in eligible ranks shall have the right to claim their own land anywhere in the overworld, given that the land has yet to be claimed. Structures, farms, containers, livestock, and other entities within the confines of this land belong to them.

(2)                The unauthorized opening and withdrawal of items inside containers constitutes theft. The unauthorized entry of structures where access to the public is revoked constitutes trespassing. The unauthorized alteration of structures—whether it be the addition or removal of blocks or spawning of harmful or destructive entities—constitutes property griefing.

(3)                Players may possess multiple owned lands in the overworld.

(4)                Property rights shall be dismissed in the Nether and End dimensions.

(5)                Players ineligible to claim their own land shall enjoy the same property rights as eligible players.

(6)                Collaborative projects must have documented ownership shares.

_Section 5._ The right of the people to information on matters of public concern shall be recognized. Access to official records, documents, and papers pertaining to official acts, transactions, or decisions shall be afforded to the people.

_Section 6._ The right of the people to form unions, associations, or societies for purposes not contrary to law shall be recognized.

_Section 7._ Players have the right to choose or create their own family to strengthen bonds with other players, offer and receive support, and progress through the game collectively.

_Section 8._ Players under the legal age shall be prohibited from participating in gambling activities. Players who introduce or influence underage players to participate in gambling activities shall be penalized.

_Section 9._ The privacy of communication and correspondence shall be inviolable. Whispered messages are sealed from the public. The Administrative Council may read only when investigating alleged crimes, or when public safety or order requires otherwise, upon lawful order of the court.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE III: THE ECONOMY</h2>

_Section 1._ The official currency of Kamote Server shall become the diamond: a rare Minecraft mineral. The exchange rate shall be fixed at $1 for every piece of diamond for playerto-player transactions. The exchange rate may be altered, by law, to adjust for inflation or other economic conflicts.

_Section 2._ All players in eligible ranks may buy, sell, and trade goods and services freely. &#x20;

_Section 3._ Players in eligible ranks may create shops that satisfy the needs of the people. Player shops may be located anywhere in the world, so long as it is accessible to other players. Goods may be delivered directly to consumers; additional fees are optional.

_Section 4._ (1) A percentage tax of 2% shall be imposed on the balance of each player, plus $1 per 1,024 active claim blocks in their claimed land, collectively referred to as “player tax.”

(2)                The collection of player tax is automated, programmed to collect at midnight of every real-life day.

(3)                The public shall have the right to be notified of the amount of money collected from them through player tax, as well as the total amount of player tax the server has collected. The notification on the total amount of collected player tax shall be issued immediately by the Administrative Council upon collection.

(4)                The mass withdrawal and hoarding of diamonds to reduce the percentage tax imposed shall be punishable by law.

(5)                Tax rates are subject to change, and the public shall be notified.

_Section 5._ The sale, purchase, or transfer of stolen property, grief tools, or exploits shall be expressly prohibited.

_Section 6._ Players may conduct informal transactions that occur outside of player shops. The player who agreed to the offer of trade must be the first to drop or submit their payment.

_Section 7._ Players shall anchor the prices of their goods upon the standard retail price provided by the State. Players may sell goods unlisted in the standard retail price according to their own price upon approval of the Administrative Council.

_Section 8._ Players shall submit proof of purchase for transactions greater than $40.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE IV: THE WORLD AND THE ENVIRONMENT</h2>

_Section 1._ The world, including its resources, minerals, animals, treasures, natural features, and naturally generated structures—unless otherwise claimed—shall be owned by the people.

_Section 2._ The placement of random blocks, digging of random holes, creation of floating trees, and other offenses that disfigure the world constitute public griefing. Players guilty of public griefing shall face fines.

_Section 3._ The terraforming of large pieces of land, mass deforestation of natural forests, flattening of mountains, and digging of large underground cavities for massive projects require an Environmental Permit.

_Section 4._ Underground tunnels dug by players shall be recognized as public grounds, unless otherwise claimed.

_Section 5._ Natural waterways, such as rivers, are protected by the State, and the construction of structures above them is prohibited, with exceptions granted when the structure to be built must absolutely require the game mechanics of rivers, such as for squid farms. In such cases, an Environmental Permit is required.

_Section 6._ Structures to be built in the middle of the ocean must be at least one hundred blocks away from the nearest shore and require an Environmental Permit. Solid land expansions on shores must be terraformed to blend in with the surrounding environment.

_Section 7._ Aerial structures must be at least fifty blocks away from the nearest claimed land of other players and require an Environmental Permit.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE V: ADMINISTRATIVE AND JUDICIAL POWERS</h2>

_Section 1._ The Administrative Council shall enforce server rules, help new players understand the game, collect player tax, process claims and permits, and preside over the court.

_Section 2._ The Administrative Council shall have access to messages in whispers and signs. The Administrative Council may investigate the nature of these messages and shall be used in court as evidence, should the nature of the messages be proven to be a threat to public safety and server security. &#x20;

_Section 3._ The Administrative Council may use emergency administrative commands amidst server plugin errors that impact the playing experience of the players negatively. The Administrative Council may enter creative mode for debugging purposes, or if the items required for debugging are hard or impossible to obtain in survival mode.

_Section 4._ The use of administrative commands to gain an unfair advantage over other players shall be punishable under administrative abuse. &#x20;

_Section 5._ The Kamorte Suprema shall be the sole and highest court in Kamote Server and shall have the exclusive power to promulgate rules concerning the protection and enforcement of constitutional rights. The Kamorte Suprema shall settle legal disputes, interpret laws, and ensure the proper administration of justice.

_Section 6._ In a trial by jury, the jury shall be chosen five minutes before a trial begins. The six jurors must not be affiliated in family with the prosecution or the defense. All six jurors must arrive at a unanimous verdict, or the trial shall otherwise be deemed a hung jury, where the defendant is neither guilty nor not guilty. In the case of a hung jury, a new trial shall commence at a later date with a new set of jurors.

_Section 7._ In administrative prosecutions, the Administrative Council in judicial roles must recuse themselves from cases where they have a personal stake. The judge and jury in administrative prosecutions shall not be citizens of the server and shall be citizens of a foreign Minecraft server in pursuance of impartiality.

_Section 8._ All judicial and administrative decisions shall be recorded, and their documents shall be available for public access.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE VI: TECHNOLOGY AND GAME MECHANICS</h2>

_Section 1._ Players may freely create farms for themselves or provide the server with goods produced from the farms.

_Section 2._ Players may use client-side mods that improve quality of life but shall refrain from mods that exploit game mechanics or circumvent server protections. &#x20;

_Section 3._ Players may use resource packs of their preference, provided they do not exploit block visibility or gain unfair advantages. &#x20;

_Section 4._ Farms found to cause excessive lag shall be dismantled after notification. &#x20;

_Section 5._ The creation of machines intended solely to cause server lag shall be prohibited. &#x20;

_Section 6._ Farms that exceed sustainable limits—defined as operations causing noticeable server performance degradation—shall be deemed too large and must be downsized. The Administrative Council shall set specific thresholds by regulation.

_Section 7._ Duplication glitches are patched by default. The discovery or abuse of new glitches shall be a punishable offense under theft by exploit. &#x20;

_Section 8._ The world border shall be set at 50,000 blocks from the world spawn point (0,0) to prevent the server disk size from getting overly bloated.

_Section 9._ The server difficulty shall be set to Hard to enhance farm efficiency and maintain challenge.



<h2 align="center">ARTICLE VII: TRANSISTORY PROVISIONS</h2>

_Section 1._ The public shall be granted seven days to accommodate to the revisions disclosed within this Constitution.

_Section 2._ All prior server rules inconsistent with this Constitution are hereby repealed.
