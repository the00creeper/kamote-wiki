# 🔃 Moving Player Ranks

### ✨ **DONATOR Role**

After successful payment and confirmation, a player may be granted the DONATOR role with:

* `/donatoradd <player>`

Likewise, when their DONATOR rank **expires**, it can be removed with:

* `/donatorremove <player>`

### 🔄 **Rank Adjustments**

Set a player’s rank: `/rankset <player> <rank>`

{% hint style="info" %}
Commonly used when players transfer to a new account.
{% endhint %}
