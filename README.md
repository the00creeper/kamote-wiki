<p align="center">We are a <strong>Philippine-based</strong> 🇵🇭 Minecraft SMP whose ideals center on preserving the <strong>vanilla aspects</strong> of the game through <strong>genuine</strong> player-to-player relationships! <h2 align="center"> What Makes Us Stand Out </h2>

{% columns %}
{% column valign="middle" %}
<h3 align="center">Our Principles 📜</h3>

<p align="center">We are committed to preserving the <strong>original vanilla experience</strong> of Minecraft, so our quality-of-life commands are light-weight. We refrain from adding overpowered features such as custom enchantments, flight commands, etc.</p>

<p align="center">Some convenient quality of life commands do exist such as <code>/home</code> and <code>tpa</code>, but are reserved for higher ranks so that new players get to experience true vanilla Minecraft first.</p>
{% endcolumn %}

{% column %}
<h3 align="center">Our Economy 🪙</h3>

<p align="center">The most iconic Kamote Server feature is its simplistic economy system. Our currency centers around the <img src=".gitbook/assets/Diamond_JE3_BE3.webp" alt="" data-size="line">, where <strong>one</strong> <img src=".gitbook/assets/Diamond_JE3_BE3.webp" alt="" data-size="line"> <strong>is equal to $1</strong> in in-game currency.</p>

<p align="center">Despite the presence of an economy, we do not have admin shops or any command-based marketplace. All shops are operated by players to preserve organic trade between them.</p>
{% endcolumn %}
{% endcolumns %}



{% columns %}
{% column %}
<h3 align="center">Our Relationships 🤝</h3>

<p align="center">We want the best experience for players and help them establish a community they can call their own. The community shall be built on cooperation, fairness, and mutual respect.</p>

<p align="center">We <strong>do not allow</strong> PvP, griefing, or stealing in the server. This is to ensure that the relationships between all players remain healthy.</p>
{% endcolumn %}

{% column %}
<h3 align="center">Our Justice System ⚖️</h3>

<p align="center">We resolve disputes and conflicts in the most objective way—by holding court trials just like in real life! Players convicted of crime is summoned to the <a href="the-justice-system/kamorte-suprema/">Kamorte Suprema</a> to face a judge and a jury and hear out their case.</p>

<p align="center">The <a href="the-justice-system/the-kamonstitution.md">Kamonstitution</a> is the server's constitution that legally binds all its laws, ideals, and principles! It deliberately explains all the technicalities on what's good and bad in the server.</p>
{% endcolumn %}
