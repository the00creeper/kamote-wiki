# ⚙️ Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

## 🔑 **Entering Moderation Mode**

Moderation commands are not accessible by default, as moderators play in Survival like regular players.

### 📝 **Procedure**

1. Execute `/mon <reason>` to enter Moderation Mode.
   * Providing a reason is mandatory.
2.  Upon activation:

    * You will enter **Spectator Mode** and become invisible to all players.
    * A Discord embed will be sent to the `#moderation-mode` channel, notifying administrators and displaying your reason.

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (2).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

    * All commands executed during this period will also be logged as embeds in the same channel.

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (3).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
3. While in Moderation Mode, you may perform necessary actions related to the stated issue.

### 🚫 **Restrictions**

During Moderation Mode, you **cannot**:

* Place or destroy blocks
* Damage players or entities
* Pick up or drop items
* Unvanish yourself

### 🔚 **Exiting Moderation Mode**

1. Execute `/moff` to exit.
2. You will return to normal Survival gameplay and lose access to moderation commands.
3.  Forward the **“has entered moderation mode”** embed from `#moderation-mode` to `#mods-journal`.<br>

    <div data-full-width="false" data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (5).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>
4.  Create a thread on the forwarded message.<br>

    <div data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (8).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>



    Inside the thread, make sure to include:

    * Screenshots or recordings
    * Results of the action taken
    * Issues requiring further review
    * Additional notes or evidence<br>

{% hint style="info" %}
All SOPs require documentation in `#mods-journal`.
{% endhint %}
