# ⛔ Rules

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/bawal yarn.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Kamote Server** heavily values player-to-player **peace** and **connection.** Offenses that **negatively impact** the experience of other players are **strictly prohibited**.&#x20;

## The Justice System ⚖️

We practice a judicial system through the oversight of the highest court, the **Kamorte Suprema,** whose purpose is to resolve server-wide disputes through **fair and impartial trials**.&#x20;

## In-Game Offenses and Sanctions ⛔

Players convicted of crime receive **server offense records**. These records **stack** among offenses and will never reset, even after season resets.

For example, a player was convicted of public griefing. The punishments for public griefing are:

* 1st Offense: 1-hour jail&#x20;
* 2nd Offense: 6-hour jail&#x20;
* 3rd Offense: Permanent ban&#x20;

Since it is his first overall server offense, he will only be sentenced to 1 hour in jail. He will also receive one server offense record.

Now, the same player was later convicted of killing another player. The punishments for killing a player are:

* 1st Offense: 6-hour jail&#x20;
* 2nd Offense: 16-hour jail&#x20;
* 3rd Offense: Permanent ban&#x20;

Because it is his second overall server offense, his previous record will **stack**. The player will instead be sentenced to 16 hours in jail.

## Chat-Based Offenses and Sanctions ⛔

These rules ensure that the server is a gentle and safe space for everyone. They apply to both in-game and Discord chats.

| Offenses                                                                                     | Sanctions                                                                                                                                                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Directly harassed other players about their gender, race, religion, or nationality.          | <ul><li>1st Offense: 24-hour mute</li><li>2nd Offense: Permanent ban</li></ul>                                                                                                      |
| Sending NSFW media such as images, GIFs, or videos.                                          | <ul><li>1st Offense: 24-hour mute</li><li>2nd Offense: Permanent ban</li></ul>                                                                                                      |
| Cursing at other players out of rage or mockery.                                             | <ul><li>1st Offense: 6-hour mute</li><li>2nd Offense: 16-hour mute</li><li>3rd Offense: Permanent ban</li></ul>                                                                     |
| Uttered derogatory words or slurs, even as a joke or without targeting anyone in particular. | <ul><li>1st Offense: 6-hour mute</li><li>2nd Offense: 12-hour mute</li><li>3rd Offense: 18-hour mute</li><li>4th Offense: 24-hour mute</li><li>5th Offense: Permanent ban</li></ul> |
| Purposefully spamming messages or media.                                                     | <ul><li>1st Offense: 6-hour mute</li><li>2nd Offense: 16-hour mute</li><li>3rd Offense: Permanent ban</li></ul>                                                                     |

{% hint style="success" %}
Cursing and light NSFW jokes are allowed in the server, as long as they don't directly attack or offend anyone.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
Despite this, players may still freely open up to us if they are being offended by this behavior, and we will act swiftly.
{% endhint %}
