# 🌟 How to Become a DONATOR

The <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/DONATOR_big.png" alt="" data-size="line"> **Rank** is available to players who wish to financially support the server’s monthly hosting costs. This system is designed to be **fair, transparent, and non–pay-to-win**, with contributions shared equally among all participating donators.

***

## Server Renewal Details 📃

* The server renews on a **30-day billing cycle**
* Monthly server cost: **470 PHP**\
  &#xNAN;_(equivalent to $7.99 USD)_
* The server was purchased on **December 11, 2025**

{% hint style="warning" %}
Because the billing cycle is every **30 days**, the renewal **will not always fall on the 11th of the month**.
{% endhint %}

The **exact renewal deadline** for each cycle will always be announced in advance on Discord to ensure all donators are informed.

***

## Dynamic Contribution System 🔁

The amount each donator contributes is **not fixed**. Instead, the total renewal cost is **divided equally among all confirmed donators for that month**.

```
Per-Donator Contribution =
₱470 ÷ Total Number of Confirmed Donators
```

This ensures:

* Fair cost sharing
* Lower individual contributions as more players donate
* No single player is pressured to shoulder the full cost

***

## Monthly Donation Timeline 📋

The donation process follows a **fixed monthly schedule** to ensure clarity and fairness.

#### 1. Donation Interest Poll (10 Days Before Renewal)

* **10 days before the renewal deadline**, a **3-day poll** is created on Discord
* Players vote to indicate whether they intend to donate for the upcoming renewal
* Voting in the poll is considered a **statement of intent**, not a payment

#### 2. Donator List Finalization (7 Days Before Renewal)

* Once the poll ends, the list of **confirmed donators is finalized**
* At this point:
  * The total number of donators is locked
  * The **per-donator contribution amount is calculated**

#### 3. Donator-Only Discord Channel

After the donator list is finalized:

* A **private Discord channel** is created for that month’s donators
* This channel is used to:
  * Announce the **final contribution amount**
  * Discuss **payment methods**
  * Share payment addresses
  * Address any questions or concerns

#### 4. Payment Window (Final 7 Days)

* Donators are given **7 days total** after finalization to make their payment
* **Payments must be completed at least 3 days before the renewal deadline**

This effectively gives:

* **4 days to pay**, with
* **3 days reserved** as a buffer before renewal

***

## Important Notes 📌

* Failure to complete payment within the required timeframe may result in:
  * Removal from the donator list for that month
  * Adjustment of the contribution amount for remaining donators
* The <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/DONATOR_big (1).png" alt="" data-size="line"> **Rank** applies **per month** and must be renewed each cycle
* Donations are strictly used for **server operation and sustainability**

***

## Transparency & Trust 🤝

* The contribution system is openly calculated and announced
* All donators can verify:
  * The total server cost
  * The number of donators
  * The resulting per-donator amount
* The <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/DONATOR_big.png" alt="" data-size="line"> **Rank** provides **quality-of-life perks only** and does not affect gameplay balance
