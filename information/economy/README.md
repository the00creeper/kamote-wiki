# 🪙 Economy

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (4).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<h2 align="center">$1 = 1 <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Diamond_JE3_BE3.webp" alt="" data-size="line"></h2>

The official currency of **Kamote Serve**r is the diamond, where **$1 = 1** <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Diamond_JE3_BE3.webp" alt="" data-size="line">**.** This means that items and services are priced based on the **value** and **effort** of obtaining diamonds. Your wealth will reflect on amount of money you have in your balance. You may use this money **buy** goods from the shops of other players!

{% hint style="info" %}
Picking up diamonds automatically converts them to their respective amount in $ to prevent [**tax evasion**](tax-system.md#automatic-diamond-conversion).
{% endhint %}

## Trading 🤝

You cannot buy items **from the server** via `/buy`; likewise, you cannot sell with `/sell`. This prevents an infinite amount of resources from flooding the server.&#x20;

Although resources in Minecraft are already theoretically infinite in quantity, player-to-player trading ensures that resources are obtained through **player effort**.&#x20;

Similarly, item auctions do not exist to give way to the establishment of player-operated [marketplaces](marketplace.md).&#x20;
