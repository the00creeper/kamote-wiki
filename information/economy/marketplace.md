# 🏪 Marketplace

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-12-14 181536.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>The <code>🏪│marketplace</code> forum channel on Discord.</p></figcaption></figure>

The **Marketplace** is a dedicated Discord forum channel where players can advertise their shops, goods, and services to the entire server community. It serves as the central hub for player-to-player trading and service offers.

All marketplace listings are posted in the `🏪│marketplace` forum channel on Discord.

***

## What Can Be Posted 📫

Players may create a marketplace post to advertise any of the following:

### Shops & Goods 🛍️

* Building materials
* Tools and weapons
* Utility items
* Food and consumables
* Rare or hard-to-obtain items

### Services 👷

* Item delivery
* Resource gathering
* Custom building services
* Mining or farming work
* Other player-offered services

Posts may include:

* A **description** of what is being sold or offered
* The **location** of the shop (coordinates or landmarks)
* **Images** of the shop, items, or completed work
* Any relevant pricing or availability details

***

## Using Tags 🏷️

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (2) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When creating a marketplace post, players must select **one or more tags** that best describe what they are offering. Tags help shoppers quickly filter listings and find exactly what they are looking for.

### Available Tags ✅

* **Building Materials** — Blocks, wood, stone, concrete, glass, etc.
* **Decorative Blocks** — For aesthetic-focused builds (terracotta, banners, etc.)
* **Enchantments** — Enchanted books and high-level gear
* **Equipment** — Tools, weapons, armor, and related gear
* **Food** — Cooked food, potions, and consumables
* **Gambling** — Player-run casinos and games of chance
* **Minerals** — Ores, ingots, gems, and raw resources
* **Mob Drops** — Bones, gunpowder, blaze rods, etc.
* **Rare Items** — Hard-to-obtain, limited, or end-game items
* **Redstone** — Components, contraptions, and automation-related items
* **Service** — Any non-item offering (delivery, labor, custom builds, etc.)

Selecting accurate tags ensures:

* Faster browsing for buyers
* Better visibility for sellers
* A more organized marketplace overall

***

## Gambling Listings 🃏

Player-run **gambling casinos** may be advertised in the Marketplace under the **Gambling** tag.

⚠️ **Important Rules**:

* **Underage players are strictly prohibited** from participating in any form of gambling ([_Kamnst. art. II, § 8_](../../the-justice-system/the-kamonstitution.md#article-ii-player-rights)).
* Violations will result in **server consequences**, which may include penalties or bans.

Players participate in gambling activities **at their own risk**.

***

## Marketplace Guidelines ℹ️

* All listings must follow server rules and community standards.
* False advertising, scams, or misleading information may result in penalties.
* Sellers are responsible for keeping their listings updated and accurate.
