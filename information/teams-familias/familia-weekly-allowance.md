# 💵 Familia Weekly Allowance

To support organized and active groups, the server provides a **weekly allowance system** funded by the server tax.

This system is designed to:

* Reward **active participation**
* Support **community-led groups**
* Prevent inactive or oversized familias from gaining unfair advantages

***

## Allowance Source & Schedule 📅

* **50% of the total tax collected for the week** is allocated to familias
* Allowances are distributed **every Saturday**
* Funds come directly from the **server account**, not from individual players

***

## Eligibility Requirements for Allowances 📜

To qualify for the weekly familia allowance, a familia must meet **all** of the following conditions:

* Must have **at least 7 members**
* The **Family Head** must be **\[PINYA] Rank** or above
* At least **4 other members** must be **\[MANGGA] Rank**
* Must have an established **Town Hall**
  * The Town Hall must be registered with the server (e.g., via Book & Quill submission)

Familias that do not meet these requirements may still exist and play normally, but will not receive weekly allowances.

## What Counts as an Active Member? ✅

Only **active members** are considered when calculating allowances.

A player is considered **active** if they:

* Played for **at least 3 hours during the week**

{% hint style="warning" %}
Inactive members are **excluded** to ensure that **active familias** are **rewarded fairly**. Smaller but active groups are not **outpaced** by large inactive ones
{% endhint %}

***

## How the Allowance Is Calculated 🧮

1. The weekly allowance is multiplied to 50% (0.5) or reduced in half
2. The resulting value is divided **to the total number of active player across all familias**
3. The quotient is multiplied back to the **number of active players in a familia** to give that familia's weekly allowance for the week
4. The computation is repeated for all familias to calculate their weekly allowance

The computation can also be represented by the function:

<h3 align="center"><span class="math">A_x=\frac{0.5T_w}{a_t}\cdot a_x</span></h3>

where:

* $$A_x$$ = Weekly allowance for Familia $$x$$
* $$T_w$$ = Weekly tax
* $$a_t$$ = Total number of active players across all familias
* $$a_x$$ = Number of active players in Familia $$x$$

## Allowance Distribution 📬

* The allowance is **not** distributed directly to individual members
* Each familia’s total allowance is given to the **Family Head (team leader)**

The Family Head is then responsible for distributing the funds internally based on their own criteria (contribution, rank, role, etc.).

This allows familias to operate with **autonomy and internal governance**.
