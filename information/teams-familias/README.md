# 🤝 Teams (Familias)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>The Shadow Empire Family | August 16, 2025</p></figcaption></figure>

On this server, we don’t use the word _“team.”_ Instead, we use **Familia** — a term that emphasizes connection, trust, and long-term bonds rather than hierarchy or competition.

## Existing Familias 🧑‍👩‍🧒

Players can view the current list of active familias in the **`👩‍👧‍👦│familia`** forum channel on Discord.

Past familias have included:

* The Crusaders
* The Dabarcats
* The Eppies
* The Herarians
* The Madelians
* The Shadow Empire

{% hint style="info" %}
We are currently starting a new season, so there may be no active familias at the moment.
{% endhint %}

***

## Joining a Familia (Paampon) 🚩

Players who wish to join a familia may request to be **paamponed** (adopted) by:

* Asking in the **`🚩│paampon`** channel, or
* Leaving a comment on the forum post of the familia they want to join

Once you are accepted:

* You gain access to the familia’s **private voice channel**
* You are officially considered part of that familia

Joining a familia is optional — players are always free to play solo.

***

## Creating Your Own Familia 🫴

Players may also establish their **own familia**.

To do so:

* Contact one of the server admins
* Provide basic information about your intended familia (name, leader, description, vision, principles, etc.)

There are **no restrictions on size, playstyle, or composition** when creating a familia.
